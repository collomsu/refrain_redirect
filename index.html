Refrain is the writing app built for songwriters and poets who take their craft seriously. Whether you're finishing a verse at midnight or stepping on stage, Refrain is the tool that goes with you.

Features

Everything you need to write and perform

Write Songs & Poems

Organize your work into songs and poems, each with their own structure. Add sections like Verse, Chorus, and Bridge to your songs, or let your poems flow freely.

Rhyme & Rhythm Tools

Built-in rhyme finder and syllable counter keep you in the flow. Spot rhymes as you write, count syllables in real time, and find the perfect word.

Performance Mode

Switch to a clean, distraction-free view of your lyrics with auto-scroll. Your eyes stay up and your words stay with you on stage.

Beautiful by Day and Night

Choose between light and dark mode, pick your favorite writing font, and make the app yours. Designed to feel as good as it looks.
